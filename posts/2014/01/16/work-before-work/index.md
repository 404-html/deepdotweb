    <p>A day&#8217;s work does not begin in the office, they began way earlier than that. Right after catching up on the day&#8217;s news, we move to stay on track with the social life of others.</p>
    <p>For me, I have exhausted quite a bit of my energy by the time I am done. Then I move to conquer the rest of the day doing some real work with what I have left.</p>
