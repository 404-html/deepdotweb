    <p>It is particularly annoying to hear ad audio in the background, but having no sight of where the ad is located in my browser. I am curious as to why websites who have videos embedded for streaming allows video ads to be on auto-play on the same page.</p>
    <p>The worst thing is that they are on repeat mode, so it never ends until it is manually turned off.</p>
