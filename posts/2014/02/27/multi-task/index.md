    <p>The definition on the ability to multi-task is extremely vague. By current logic, anyone who can perform two or more task at the same time is considered to be multi-tasking. In literally terms, that means eating while watching television is effectively a multi-tasking skill. Although, I wouldn&#8217;t know how effective it is in performing work-related objectives.</p>
