    <p>I was already a teen when I first discovered Happy Tree Friends.</p>
    <p>There was a hype going around that prompted me to catch a few episodes. I was surprised, naturally. The title was just too misleading for anyone to expect what I saw from simple cartoons. I will be honest that I watched quite a few clips, but parents should definitely identify the true nature of the show.</p>
