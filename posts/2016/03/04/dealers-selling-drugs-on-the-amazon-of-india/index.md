    </div><span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25"></span><p>Purchasing drugs on the darknet? Why do that when you can purchase from “<a href="http://www.indiamart.com/">IndiaMart</a>”?</p>
    <p>On IndiaMart, you will find vendors selling everything from illegal drugs to old movies.</p>
    <p>One would think that a market like IndiaMart wouldn&#8217;t be successful but they&#8217;re worth $146 million.</p>
    <p>IndiaMart, currently employing 2600 people, is one of India&#8217;s largest online marketplace.</p>
    <p>Not counting the legitimate sellers, IndiaMart is filled to the brim with drug dealers and scammers.</p>
    <p>Likewise to the darknet, these vendors don&#8217;t care about legal repercussions.</p>
    <p>On IndiaMart, you will find a <em>designated</em> policy against contraband, yet the vendors are still active.</p>
    <p>One vendor, “Daiichi”, made the statement “We don’t really have police trouble from this. &#8230;”</p>
    <p>According to a <a href="http://www.globalpost.com/article/6735091/2016/02/18/indiamart-drugs">report</a> by GlobalPost, “If someone will buy it, someone is likely selling it on IndiaMart, the second-largest online wholesale marketplace on the planet in terms of the sheer number of products for sale.”</p>
    <p>The report compares IndiaMart to <a href="http://www.alibaba.com/">Alibaba</a> who has a “cleaner” image.</p>
    <p>“Alibaba, its main competitor, appears almost entirely free of offers for illegal drugs. So do eBay and Amazon. Meanwhile, IndiaMart, despite a stated <a href="http://www.indiamart.com/terms-of-use.html">policy</a> against posting illegal goods, hosts hundreds of vendors promising bulk shipments of controlled substances.”</p>
    <p>Sumit Bedi, Vice President of Marketing for IndiaMart, said that the vendors are using slang that they and government are having a hard time figuring out which is why drug dealing is so widespread on IndiaMart.</p>
    <p>However, GlobalPost pointed out that this is false, “But the drugs offered on IndiaMart are hardly hidden behind a maze of impenetrable code. Want to shop for methamphetamine on IndiaMart? Simply type “meth” in the search bar and dozens of sellers are vying for your cash.”</p>
    <p>The vendor mentioned earlier, Daiichi – who sells meth, continued in his statement, “I know this is a controlled substance, But we’re very discreet. We ship worldwide. We are verified sellers and you can even confirm that with IndiaMart.”</p>
    <p>Being “verified” shouldn&#8217;t mean much since the only thing you need to do to become a verified seller is attach your email address to your account. Due to the easy verification requirements, scamming is rampant.</p>
    <p>Bedi promises to work on purging drug dealers and scammers, saying IndiaMart has “a responsibility to the market”. Although Mukesh Choudhary, a cybercrime expert who helps train law enforcement in India, has said told GlobalReport that “anyone dumb enough to file a police complaint against an online drug dealer, he says, faces imprisonment for soliciting narcotics”.</p>
