    <p>How about that? Forget the auto shop. You can purchase any defective parts from the next grocery stall you find. If it does the job, it works.</p>
    <p>We have been misled into purchasing branded, expensive auto parts to replace the faulty ones. Pringles (above) seems to work just as well. You can even devour the contents first.</p>
