</div><span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25"></span><p><a href="http://www.deepdotweb.com/marketplace-directory/listing/the-free-market">The Free Market</a> admins announced on <a href="http://www.reddit.com/r/DarkNetMarkets/comments/2vzdjc/marketupdate_the_freemarket_new_features_added/">Reddit</a> (join the discussion there) about new features added to the market:</p>
<div class="usertext-body may-blank-within md-container">
<div class="md">
<blockquote><p>The Free Market has now been online for one month. We are now experiencing more vendor sign ups and expecting to see more listings become available to buyers over the next few weeks.</p>
<p>Experienced and reputable vendors can still have the registration fee waived by submitting a support request before they register their account.</p>
<p>Some of the new features that have been implemented over this past month:</p>
<ul>
<li>Private stealth authenticated URL&#8217;s for vendors</li>
<li>Automated delivery of electronic items</li>
<li>Currency conversions</li>
<li>Shopping cart to order multiple items at once from a vendor</li>
<li>No images mode</li>
<li>Multiple color schemes</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional features being developed include;</p>
<ul>
<li>Anonymous buyer score/ratings tracking</li>
<li>Responsive design</li>
<li>Limited access accounts for vendors</li>
<li>Promoted listings</li>
<li>Tiered commission structure</li>
</ul>
<p>During this one month period we have averaged a response time of less than 20 minutes on every support request submitted. We have also improved the hardware our service is deployed upon to ensure a smooth and reliable level of service.</p>
<p>Sales commission is now 2% for the next 2 months and reputable vendors can have the application fee waived.</p>
<p>http://tfmarket6iaddx45.onion</p></blockquote>
