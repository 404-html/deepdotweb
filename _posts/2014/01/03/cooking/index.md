    <p>I simply have no talent in cooking. I remember when I used to watch some Gordon Ramsay cooking lessons on Youtube and then felt confident enough to set out and conquer the kitchen.</p>
    <p>Definitely a lesson I will not repeat again. Salmon could be uncooked then served as sushi and will still taste pretty good. I have no idea why mine could actually taste bad after I tried to pan-grilled them.</p>
