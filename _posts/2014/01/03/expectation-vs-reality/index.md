    <p>&nbsp;</p>
    <p>There is a specific look that applies well for every individual. Just because some movie star made it look cool doesn&#8217;t mean we should jump into those looks.</p>
    <p>It is a sad fact that things don&#8217;t always turn out the way we want them to. The last thing we want is to invest into grooming ourselves into a look that doesn&#8217;t suit us, and then getting stuck in it until the everyone gets the laugh out of their system.</p>
