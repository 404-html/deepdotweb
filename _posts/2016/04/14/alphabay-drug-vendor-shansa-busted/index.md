    <p>31-year-old (not named by authorities) Viennese man, better known as Shansa on the dark web, <a href="http://derstandard.at/2000034526427/Wiener-Dealer-betrieb-internationalen-Drogenversand-im-Internet">was busted</a> by the Bundeskriminalamt (BK) on 13 October 2015. Authorities seized 2.8 kg of amphetamines, 1 kilogram of ecstasy, cocaine, LSD blotters and various kinds of research drugs (RCs) in the dealer’s house. Police also found illegal guides on the man’s computer along with a tableting machine, which could be used to produce ecstasy and speed pills.</p>
    <p>Shansa had 182 international drug sales, he sold illegal substances to Europe, the United States and even to Australia and India. He also had 73 domestic transactions from Austrian buyers. Within the period of just 5 months, the dark net market vendor had made around 15,000 Euro from sales, according to a report by the law enforcement authorities.</p>
