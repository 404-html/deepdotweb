    <p>September 23, Kiev will host <a href="http://goo.gl/8Mkgx5">Blockchain &amp; Bitcoin Conference 2016.</a> One of its topics will include the development of cryptocurrency exchange platforms. Benjamin Bommhardt, a cofounder and Sales Director at Draglet, will speak within the Fintech section.</p>
    <p><a href="https://www.deepdotweb.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/exchangerisks.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15404" src="https://www.deepdotweb.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/exchangerisks.jpg" alt="exchangerisks" width="800" height="500" srcset="https://www.deepdotweb.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/exchangerisks.jpg 800w, https://www.deepdotweb.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/exchangerisks-300x188.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 800px) 100vw, 800px" /></a></p>
    <p>Participants of the expert section will discuss three components of cryptocurrency platforms operating: development, challenges and prospects. The speaker will share his opinion regarding the current situation and tell about risks from the perspective of exchange platform owners.</p>
    <p><strong>Benjamin Bommhardt </strong>is the expert in crypto trading area and cofounder of Munich-based Draglet (the company develops and tests software for digital currencies exchanges). Besides presentation at industry events, he also gives Bitcoin lecturers atÂ Munich University of Applied Sciences (MUAS).</p>
    <p>The expert section will also include presentations dedicated to cryptocurrency legislative regulation issues, Bitcoin application in the banking sector and Blockchain technologies in public administration.</p>
    <p>Along with the Pro section, there will be master classes for developers and educative lecturers for those who want to know more about Bitcoin and blockchain.</p>
    <p>The conference will take place at the Cosmopolit congress hall on 23 September.</p>
    <p>Details and registration on the website: <a href="http://goo.gl/8Mkgx5">Blockchain &amp; Bitcoin Conference 2016.</a></p>
