<p>We hate the idea of having bulges on our body.</p>
<p>A fat wallet, however, is a bulge we definitely wouldn&#8217;t mind. The sense of self satisfaction you get from having this lovable bulge is a feeling that is hard to deny. It used to be wallets stuff with notes, but have been replaced with wallets stuffed with cards.</p>
<p>It&#8217;s all the same to me though, I am happy either way.</p>
