    <p>Normal social cultures requires us to be careful with our social exchanges. It doesn&#8217;t pay to be overly frank, and then get criticized for being insensitive. A good way to save us all that trouble is to mind the questions we ask, so we can rid the world of obvious lies and make communications more honest.</p>
