<p>I still don&#8217;t get how this design flop manage to pass any logical thinking.</p>
<p>This is basically changing for the sake of changing. I can find no special benefits for having the touch pad placed on top of the keyboard. It will simply change the way I do things, with no real advantage.</p>
<p>Simply put, it is a unnecessary hassle.</p>
