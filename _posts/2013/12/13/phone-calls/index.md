</div><span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25"></span><p><a href="http://www.deepdotweb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMAGE98.jpg" rel="lightbox"> <img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2176" alt="Instant Messages" src="https://www.deepdotweb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMAGE98.jpg" width="468" height="468"/> </a>Instant messages (and I don&#8217;t mean SMSes), have replaced the need of communicating via a voice call. There are obvious benefits such as able to keep a record/log of what was discussed and a selection of emoticons to convey emotions sometimes incomprehensible by voice.</p>
<p>There are so few occasions now that would actually require me to call someone else.</p>
