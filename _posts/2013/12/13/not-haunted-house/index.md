<p>Oh wow, finally found a house that is not haunted.</p>
<p>As someone who have years of sales experience, I know to abide to the rule of not speaking whatever doesn&#8217;t contribute to the desired outcome. That would mean that I don&#8217;t touch on topics that does not benefit me.</p>
<p>Surely this sign will scare more potential buyers away if there are rumours to begin with in the first place.</p>
