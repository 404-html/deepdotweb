</div><span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25"></span><p>I guess it is fairly safe to say that none of these people are real experts in their respective martial arts. Some of them seem like they have never practised it before trying to record these attempts on camera. A lot of them actually look like they could do it. I guess some practitioners of martial arts today are trying harder to look good than to actually master the skills.</p>
