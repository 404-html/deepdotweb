</div><span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25" ></span><p>This is a problem worldwide. Phone companies, who are logically expected to handle enormously amount of traffic on their customer service hotlines, are seemingly always experiencing a lack of staff.</p>
<p>This leads me to wonder if it is all a tactic to increase waiting time so you will surrender your opportunity to complain unless it is truly what you will want to see through.</p>
