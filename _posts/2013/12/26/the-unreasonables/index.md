<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>There are some people who will try to find faults at every turn. They have been allowed to be so critical of others that sometimes their demands can be just overly unreasonable.</p>
<p>Service providers should at some point allow their representatives to bluntly point out if a customer is being unreasonable. Some people just need to be educated, or they will be allowed to breed a new generation of madness.</p>
