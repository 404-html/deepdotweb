<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The best thing about working for yourself is that you no longer need to feel mistreated and undervalued. I can be earning peanuts, but at least I am doing it for myself. It is a better feeling than that of battling sharks for huge companies only to get pain in tunas and sardines.</p>
<p>It is not easy to determine how much we can earn, but it will feel a lot better if you know who you are fighting for.</p>
