    <p>As the cost of living rises, the average person may begin to wonder how long can his savings last if he should become unemployed. Those who are considering to retire have to take cautious steps to ensure they are ready for what is to come. It will most certainly be a nightmare to retire and then realise the savings is not going to last long enough.</p>
