<p>I can get drunk on beer, and always end up safely at home. On tequila shots though, its an entirely different story. Everytime I get drunk on shots, I perceive that every place is a comfy place to rest. Fortunately, I almost always ended up sleeping in some fairly clean toilet cubicles.</p>
<p>I can&#8217;t say the same for my friends though.</p>
