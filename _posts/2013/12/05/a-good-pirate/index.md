<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>I think children should be taught the way things really are. At the very least, try not to entirely modify a definition into something that sounds better. What are pirates who don&#8217;t rob? It is like saying a good thief never steals.</p>
<p>If values is the concern, use a different occupation. Why would one even use pirates as an example to impart values?</p>
