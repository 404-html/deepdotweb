<p>When I came across this comment, I stopped to imagine what I would do if I am really on fire.</p>
<p>Will I remember these instructions, or will I try to use my bare hands to flap out the fire. I did finally came to a conclusion.</p>
<p>If I am ever on fire, I will be pretty much a dead man.</p>
