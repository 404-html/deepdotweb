</div><span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25" ></span><p>This video got a lot of hype when it was posted on Youtube. Got lots of negative comments criticizing the family for &#8216;emotionally abusing&#8217; by making fun of the daughter&#8217;s tantrum.</p>
<p>This is what wrong with society, when people exhibit huge amount of anxiety over many harmless things, and neglecting those that are truly important. I personally feel this is a good example of making light out of a bad situation, nothing wrong with that.</p>
