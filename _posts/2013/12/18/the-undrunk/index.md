    <p>There is a good reason why it is not the best idea to have someone who doesn&#8217;t drink to join in session of drinking.</p>
    <p>Chances are things may get weird, and possibly downright stupid. It is a good thing if the whole bunch of us are drunk, we can safely laugh about it the next day without feeling too bad about it.</p>
    <p>It is a different idea, if we have someone around to witness the stupidity that he doesn&#8217;t understands.</p>
