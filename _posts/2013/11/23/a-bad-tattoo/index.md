<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Tattoos can be a display of your individuality. But what if, you get the wrong tattoo artist?</p>
<p>If this tattoo was meant to emit a sense of realism, then I would say it has failed considerably badly. That&#8217;s why I always tell myself if I ever wanted a tattoo, it will be focused much on conceptual art. I can justify bad art with endless amounts of justifications by insisting they are from an artistic perspectives.</p>
<p>A bad photo, is however, just a bad photo.</p>
