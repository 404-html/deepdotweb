</div><span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25" ></span><p>Came across this on Maddox, funny life-lessons that adults should impart.</p>
<p>Here are a few more:</p>
<ul>
<li>You don&#8217;t get to use sarcasm. Adults invented sarcasm for other adults.</li>
<li>Quit trying to manipulate others with your tears.</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t talk during movies.</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t talk during car rides.</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t talk while music is playing.</li>
<li>Don&#8217;t talk.</li>
</ul>
