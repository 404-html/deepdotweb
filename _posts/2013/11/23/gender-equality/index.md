    <p>How can total equality exist?</p>
    <p>Let&#8217;s face it, both genders have their general strengths and weakness. There are things that are undeniable more suitable for a specific gender. As far as equality is concerned, most parts of the world open equal opportunities for both genders. As far as the individual is concerned, it is always subjective and will remain so.</p>
