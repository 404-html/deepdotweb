<p>How do you make difficult decisions? Tossing a coin or doing it the seemingly logical way &#8211; Weighing the pros &amp; cons?</p>
<p>Well, I have on multiple times led myself into thinking rational thinking produces the best results. Therefore, I choose to weigh the consequences of the available options with a list. However, life is not as easy as we want it to be and this is not how it works. Whatever methods we chose to determine our decision, the truth is that outcomes are rarely within our control.</p>
<p>We would like to THINK that everything can be rationalized, but we probably couldn&#8217;t do any worse than flipping a coin.</p>
