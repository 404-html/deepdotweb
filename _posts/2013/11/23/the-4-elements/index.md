<p>In Thailand, a mini-sized bag of Lays costs 10 baht. Expectedly, it is not very expensive since you are mainly paying for the packaging. Inside the bag, contains less than 1/3 of the contents that you actually want. The others is just air.</p>
<p>So I guess, if it is the contents of the bag that I am paying for, I am actually paying for air.</p>
