<p>Over the years, I find a desire to change my look every now and then. It has now become clear that there is no chance I will look good with facial hair. I either look way past my age (in a bad way) or I simply look messy.</p>
<p>I envy those who look naturally good with that patch of beautiful facial hair. You are a true man.</p>
