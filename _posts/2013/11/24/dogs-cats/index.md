<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>It has occurred to me that dogs don&#8217;t actually hate cats. The fundamental reason that they can&#8217;t get along is due to a difference of social style. Dogs have no qualms about going up close and sniffing up something they have a curiosity for, and cats are seemingly afraid of anything that comes close to them.</p>
<p>And when the cats start running, the dogs see it as the beginning of a game of chase.</p>
