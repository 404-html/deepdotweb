<p>I sometimes try exceptionally hard to look as normal as possible when I approach a lone individual, and the factor is automatically amplified it is a girl. I do realise that this behaviour is a sub-conscious one, as I also do realise it was unnecessary and made me look eccentric.</p>
<p>That is why my phone is now my best friend in these scenarios.</p>
