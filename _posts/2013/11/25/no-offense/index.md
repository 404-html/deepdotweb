<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>We have cultivated a habit of saying &#8220;No Offense&#8221;, when we are about to say something unpleasant to the intended receiver. The truth is, it is going to offend the other party as little as they would admit it. If you have to do it, do it in a forthright manner. Otherwise, don&#8217;t.</p>
<p>It wouldn&#8217;t make a difference.</p>
