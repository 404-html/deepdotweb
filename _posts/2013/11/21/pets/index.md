<p>I have came across people who places huge emphasis into integrating the existence of their beloved pets into the practices of human.</p>
<p>Some even create Facebook pages with regular updates. My opinion is that such practices have no value to the pets in questions, all they ever needed was your attention. Think about it for a second, do you think they (your pets) would be happier if you utilize the time spent on updating your pet&#8217;s profile page into actually spending time with them?</p>
