<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Too many people out there, both atheists and the religious, are out to prove each other wrong. A belief is an individual&#8217;s way of life, and it is beyond me why that should impact another. This is a subject that everyone knows will be hard to substantiate by evidence, so why even bother?</p>
<p>Live your life the way you choose to, and stay out of others.</p>
