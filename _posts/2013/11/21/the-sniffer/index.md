<p>I always wondered why dogs have the tendency to vigorously smell the same spot over and over again. I know the whole point is to find the same old spot where it last done business, but why does he need to re-check so many times over?</p>
<p>Correct me if I wrong, I think they just love to reminisce their past glorious excretions.</p>
