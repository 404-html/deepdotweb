    <p>It amuses me when I wonder why advertisements can contain misspelled words. I have came across countries where English is not a first language, and yet poster advertisements were written in it.</p>
    <p>Secondly, why would fresh vegetable juice warrant an advertisement of this scale? Wherever this is, it makes me feel like I can even make fresh water sound like quite a buy.</p>
