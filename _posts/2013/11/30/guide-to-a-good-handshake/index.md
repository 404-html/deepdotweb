</div><span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25" ></span><p>Handshakes are the concluding gesture of a closed deal.</p>
<p>It leaves a good impression if you deliver a good one. However, there are a thousand ways to do it wrong. Watch this video as it shows you the what-nots of a good handshake. The wet fish is commonly seen, but have you ever encountered the Granddad or the Scobe?</p>
