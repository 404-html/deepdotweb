<p>If there are ever powers beyond our understanding, I am sure they have no place on the internet.</p>
<p>I have seen chain mails circulating ever since I was a kid. One would think that education will eventually wipe them out. I am not surprised when I see new chain messages, I am just surprised there are people who circulate due to fear for their safety.</p>
<p>Seriously?</p>
