<p>I have led myself to believe that IKEA furniture were something that I could assemble quickly. And I was always proven wrong.</p>
<p>Perhaps, I am just to stingy to fork out that extra 50. Because I always swear that I would use their assembling services the next time round, but I always end up trying to do it myself time and again.</p>
