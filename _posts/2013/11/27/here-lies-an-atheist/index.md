<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>If there are no gods, then there is no heaven to speak of.</p>
<p>If atheists are buried after they die, what is the point? If its just for the memory to remember them by, cremating them works just the same. They have nowhere to be except in memories.</p>
<p>It is quite sad though, to face eternal nothingness after death.</p>
