    <p>We hear people say that money is not everything all the time. It is comforting, but that&#8217;s all it serves.</p>
    <p>Nothing is everything. That is a fact. It is also a fact that money can get you a lot of those things that you really need. Fundamentally, you need food. Anyone knows where we can get food everyday without money. What about better foods, healthier foods? I hear they are a path to better health, and I hear they cost more.</p>
    <p>So yes, money is pretty much everything.</p>
