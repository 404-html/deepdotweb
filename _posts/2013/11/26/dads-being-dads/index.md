<p>Being a dad is a wonderful title, as a father of one or more children. But when it comes to babysitting, dads are simply not as good as the mums.</p>
<p>Men are not programmed genetically to handle babysitting work. Sure, it can be done, just not as well. Well, men do well when there&#8217;s instruction books. Make one, maybe?</p>
