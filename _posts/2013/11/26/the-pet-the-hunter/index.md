</div><span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25"></span><p><a href="http://www.deepdotweb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMAGE48.jpg" rel="lightbox"> <img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1854" alt="Spider Hunter" src="https://www.deepdotweb.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMAGE48.jpg" width="460" height="639"/> </a>Dogs have a tendency to over-react. An itch will quickly initiate into a ritualistic dance, and anyone at the door is automatically tagged as enemy of state. The ensuring mess forms the aftermath of event we can perhaps reluctantly label as an incident. It is amazing how some things that are hardly significant in a house without dogs are so amplified in a house with one.</p>
