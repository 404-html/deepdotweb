    <p>Self obsession. That&#8217;s what I would call it.</p>
    <p>There is a part of us that constantly about how others will look at us, and then proceed to constantly judge us on everything we do. Then I realise we all live for ourselves. And humans are selfish beings, we probably spend more time thinking for ourselves than we do for other people.</p>
    <p>So why even bother?</p>
