<p>A superhuman possesses abilities beyond that of normal men. What if, the abilities were reversed and a spider is bestowed with the abilities of a common man. That would pretty much render it kinda useless, right?</p>
<p>Be happy with what you were given, there is no guarantee you will be happier when you have the abilities of someone else. You are who you are, and what you have is what made you special.</p>
