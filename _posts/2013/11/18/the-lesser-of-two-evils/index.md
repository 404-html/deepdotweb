    <p>&nbsp;</p>
    <p>We were brought up being taught right from wrong. As we grow older, we discover that many things are not as simple as they sound. The irony of life is that we are often presented with choices that do not have a clear moral definition. These choices, fortunately or unfortunately, lead us to different places.</p>
    <p>We can only hope that where we are now and where we will be is the result of a choice less bad.</p>
