<p>Not exactly the best thing to do, but I wish people would adopt a lighter perspective on occasional misuse.</p>
<p>In some countries, particularly Asian countries in my experience, people take a serious view in privileges reserved for the disabled. You could literally see empty reserved seats even during peak hours on a subway ride. While I strongly believe in their uses, I do feel that common sense can be exercised when the average guy uses them.</p>
<p>It&#8217;s OK to use them, just give them up if you see someone who needs it more.</p>
