    <p>&nbsp;</p>
    <p>As we grow, the things that matter to us changes.</p>
    <p>As kids, things that matter were just candies and toys. As we approach adulthood, we worry about expenses and car payments. When age catches up with us, our priorities will change again. We thought we had a grasp on the things of the world, but later we realise it is them that have a grasp on us all along.</p>
