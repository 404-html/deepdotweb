    <p>&nbsp;</p>
    <p>For the party animals out there, this is almost routine. When the alcohol starts flowing, the grip on our wallet loosens and our decisions become increasingly questionable. I do suppose that&#8217;s why we will probably do it all over again, because that&#8217;s perhaps part of the fun.</p>
    <p>It&#8217;s good to be able to thoroughly enjoy ourselves, and tolerate the inevitable consequences later. That&#8217;s just life, isn&#8217;t it?</p>
