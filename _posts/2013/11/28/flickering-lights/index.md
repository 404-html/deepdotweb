<p>I spend a lot of my time alone, in a 2-storey house with bedrooms more than the total occupants. When I&#8217;m alone, I sometimes get illogical. I can hardly fix a light bulb, so forgive me when a part of me attribute flickering lights to demons and ghosts. Of course, I don&#8217;t entirely believe. But it doesn&#8217;t help in comforting me when I am sitting alone in the dark.</p>
