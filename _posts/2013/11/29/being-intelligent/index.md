<p>How do we define being intelligent?</p>
<p>Are age and life experiences a factor in determining one&#8217;s intelligence? Or are we simply born with the intellect we possess. Some people are smart, and others act smarter than they really are. I believe in one thing. Being exposed to more life experiences doesn&#8217;t make one smarter. It is what we do when these events unfold, that defines us and our intelligence.</p>
