<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>We try to look good in our resumes, since that&#8217;s what potential employers would like to see. We can be honest about pretty much everything like previous occupations and education. But the skills section, I find it particularly doubtful that anyone should fully take another&#8217;s word for it. These are pretty much very general descriptions and if they are 100% true, then that makes most of us quite flawless.</p>
